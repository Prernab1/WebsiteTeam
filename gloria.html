About me

I am Gloria, a young girl with innocent doe eyes but spirit of a tiger. I come from Italy but I am currently living in London to study. I am on my 3rd year of BA Business Management with Marketing and I wish to continue with a Master in Business Administration or Data analysis. I am currently working as a Sales agent and B2B marketing assistant for a company that designs and manufactures hydraulic accessories for doors - not a very feminine industry - but I like the role I am in as it allows me to promote their products, make effective presentations, negotiate with possible clients and conduct market research. What I love about marketing is the analytical part of it; the strategy, identification, planning and analysis of the qualitative and quantitative data available.
